What is FlowMates?

Flowmates -is a global community of friendly and collaborative people with one common goal - developing open-source technologies.
Our core mission is to create the best conditions for business digitalization with the help of open source low-code platform and training of citizen developers.

What is the conference about?

Flowmates invite all tech specialists, geeks no/low-code evangelists, IT communities, investors, or investment funds to take part in the forthcoming event. Dedicated to the battle of approaches to the implementation of projects using No-Code, the event will be useful for both investors and IT innovators interested in taking the maximum advantages out of the IT revolution.
Ukraine bank representatives and No-Code communities will be presented at the conference where the professional speakers will discuss No-Code solutions for business, successful cases, and fuckups.
