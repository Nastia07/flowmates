What is FlowMates?

Flowmates -is a global community of friendly and collaborative people with one common goal - developing open-source technologies. Our core mission is to create the best conditions for business digitalization with the help of open source low-code platform and training of citizen developers.

What is the conference about?

Flowmates invite all tech specialists, geeks no/low-code evangelists, IT communities, investors, or investment funds to take part in the forthcoming event. Dedicated to the battle of approaches to the implementation of projects using No-Code, the event will be useful for both investors and IT innovators interested in taking the maximum advantages out of the IT revolution. Ukraine bank representatives and No-Code communities will be presented at the conference where the professional speakers will discuss No-Code solutions for business, successful cases, and fuckups.

How to become a citizen
developer?

Citizen Developer - a specialist who creates IT solutions without an IT/engineering education. Such specialists are switchers who were able to master No-Code and become part of the IT sector.

Leave a request below and join the first Ukrainian No-Code community!

By joining as a Citizen Developer you:

  • receive training and discounts on events in the FlowMates community

  • you will have a strong community in which hundreds of other specialists are striving for professional self-development

  • get exclusive access to vacancies from companies contributing to the community
